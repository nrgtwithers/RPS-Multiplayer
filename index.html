Rock, Paper, Scissors!

  • detailsDirections
    1. First, you start the game by submit your name to define, which player you are.
    2. You and the second player will then click on one of the images on the bottom. You can choose the rock, paper, or scissors.
    3. Main rules are: Rock wins against scissors. Scissors win against paper. Paper wins against rock. If you both click on the same choice it will be a tie.
    4. You both can play as many times as you'd like, but must both be connected to play.
    5. You can also, feel free to message each other in the messaging system.
